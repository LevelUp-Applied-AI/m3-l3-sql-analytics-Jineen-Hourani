# KPI Brief — Levant Tech Solutions

## KPI 1: Average Salary per Employee

**Definition:**
Average salary across all employees calculated using the employees table.

**Current value:**
63200

**Interpretation:**
This indicates the overall compensation level in the company and helps benchmark salary competitiveness.


## KPI 2: Department Salary Efficiency

**Definition:**
Total salary per department divided by the number of employees in that department.

**Current value:**
Engineering has the highest total salary at 938000.

**Interpretation:**
Shows how efficiently each department is utilizing its salary budget relative to its workforce size.


## KPI 3: Employee Utilization Rate

**Definition:**
Percentage of employees assigned to at least one project.

**Current value:**
70%

**Interpretation:**
Indicates how effectively employees are engaged in active projects. A lower percentage may suggest underutilized resources.
